# Staff Guide

Staff access is operational and branch-aware.

Staff can access only pages allowed by:

- `user_profiles.role`
- `user_profiles.branch_id`
- `role_permissions`
- enabled modules
- Supabase RLS

Staff may work with assigned operational records such as bookings, customers, POS, and staff-specific records when permissions allow.

Staff cannot:

- Access full Data Backup
- Export full business data
- Manage modules
- Access Owner-only settings
- Read another business data
