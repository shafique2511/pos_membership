# User Guide

Luxantara Members has four roles:

- Owner
- Manager
- Staff
- Customer / Member

## Dashboard Users

Owners, Managers, and Staff use `/dashboard`. Visible pages depend on role, branch access, and enabled modules.

## Customer Portal

Customers use `/portal`. Customer records must be linked to `customers.user_id`.

Customers can:

- View their dashboard
- Book appointments, tables, rooms, or resources
- View booking history
- View memberships and QR member card
- View rewards and points
- View payment history and receipts when enabled
- Update their profile

Customers cannot access business backup, staff, inventory, POS admin, or reports.
