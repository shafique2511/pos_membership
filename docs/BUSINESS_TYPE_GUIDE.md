# Business Type Guide

Supported business types:

- Barber Shop
- Coffee Shop
- Salon
- Spa
- Clinic
- Event Space
- Custom Business

Business type controls wording and recommended setup. It does not permanently restrict modules.

## Business Types With Presets

- Barber Shop
- Coffee Shop
- Clinic
- Custom Business

## Business Types Without Presets

- Salon
- Spa
- Event Space

Salon, Spa, and Event Space remain supported in booking, membership, POS, inventory, staff, payments, reports, and customer portal workflows. They use manual module selection.
